Part 2: Multiple Choice Explanations

Question 21: B
正确选项证据 Phil说 "I try, but I don't think I do enough. So, maybe I cycle more than I drive, and I try to recycle things, but I should be doing a lot more."（第12行）。他承认自己有所行动但仍不够，与B选项"承认行动不足但有所努力"完全吻合。
错误选项分析 A: 明确说"I don't think I do enough"，并非感到自豪(proud)。C: 他积极回应并列举了行为，并非漠不关心(indifferent)。D: 原文未提及对他人感到沮丧(frustrated)。
Question 22: D
正确选项证据 Hannah说 "we can't just rely on individuals... We need to actually change the way our economy works."（第15行）。"change the way our economy works"即"结构性经济转型"，与D选项吻合。
错误选项分析 A: 未提及法律责任(legally responsible)。B: 并非将责任归咎于政府(alone to blame)。C: 并非说个人行为"完全无关"(completely irrelevant)，属于极端表达误导。
Question 23: A
正确选项证据 Hannah说 "traditionally governments have always wanted to grow the economy. More money, more jobs and more stuff."（第31行）。"more money, more jobs and more stuff"对应A选项的"output, employment, and material wealth"。
错误选项分析 B/C/D: 均为概念偷换或信息错位。鼓励减少消费是degrowth的主张，而非传统政府目标。
Question 24: C
正确选项证据 Alvaro说 "degrowth is not saying that we need to reduce everything. It's saying that we need to decrease some elements of our production that are not beneficial."（第40行）。"some elements... not beneficial"对应C选项的"specific harmful sectors"。
错误选项分析 A: 明确说"not reduce everything"，"完全消除"是极端表达。B: 举例不仅限交通。D: 未提及政府控制产出。
Question 25: B
正确选项证据 Phil说 "Fast fashion refers to clothes that are made very cheaply and quickly, so that customers can afford to buy new outfits more often."（第49行）。B选项是对原文的同义替换。
错误选项分析 A: 未提及回收材料。C: 并非限制供应(limits supply)。D: 聚焦于生产方式，而非社交媒体传播。
Question 26: D
正确选项证据 Hannah说 "...Some of them argue that it would create more poverty. And some economists believe degrowth would actually make it harder to solve the climate crisis."（第52行）。D选项是对这两点批评的综合同义替换。
错误选项分析 A/B/C: 货币政策、国际贸易、金融市场稳定等均未在文中提及，属于无中生有。
Question 27: A
正确选项证据 Fankhauser说 "Degrowth underestimates the power of innovation... the need for investment that we need in new technology and in new solutions."（第58行）。"technological progress"对应"innovation"。
错误选项分析 B/C: 政府监管、国际合作未提及。D: 批评的是低估创新，而非高估可再生能源转型速度。
Question 28: C
正确选项证据 Phil说 "Another factor that Professor Fankhauser says is underestimated by degrowthers is the feasibility of social change."（第64行）。"realistic likelihood... can be carried out"是"feasibility"的同义替换。
错误选项分析 A/B/D: 税收意愿、发展中国家、文化差异等均未提及。
Question 29: D
正确选项证据 Phil说 "For example, I could say that I think it's feasible for us to finish this podcast in six minutes. I think that we can do it!"（第64行）。这是Phil在解释"feasible"一词时用播客本身举例，属于用日常情境说明词义。
错误选项分析 A/B/C: Phil举例纯粹为了解释词汇，并非质疑degrowth，也非真的担心超时。
Question 30: B
正确选项证据 全文呈现了支持degrowth的观点（Hannah、Alvaro）与反对意见（多位经济学家、Fankhauser），形成明显对立。说明该概念仍具争议性(contested)。
错误选项分析 A: 明确指出many economists do not think it is a good idea，并未转向支持。C: 未提及政府已采纳。D: 争议不仅限于行业缩减，还涉及贫困、创新、社会可行性等。
