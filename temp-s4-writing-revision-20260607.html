📋 Article vs Essay — 核心区别

两者都是 DSE Paper 2 Part B 常见题型，但写法不同：

比较点 Article 文章 Essay 议论文
目的 分享观点、资讯、经验，可带娱乐性 论证一个立场，说服读者
语气 较轻松，可半正式 正式、客观
读者 杂志/校报读者，较广泛 考官 / 指定读者
标题 必须有吸引眼球的标题 通常题目已给出
首段 用 hook 吸引读者 清晰表明立场 (thesis)
举例 个人故事、趣闻皆可 数据、事实、逻辑论证为主

⏱ 考试时间分配建议

5 min — 审题，确认文体，brainstorm 3个主要论点
5 min — 写 outline（段落结构）
30 min — 正式写作
5 min — 检查语法、串联词、标题格式

📊 评分标准 (DSE Marking)

Content 内容 论点是否充分、有说服力？
Language 语言 词汇、语法、句式是否多样准确？
Organisation 结构 段落是否清晰，是否有串联词？

三者并重，目标每项达到 Level 4 = 合格。Level 5+ 需要语言亮点。

Article 文章写法

常见题型

为学校杂志/社区报/旅游网站写文章，题目如：

  • Write an article about a local problem that needs attention.
  • Write an article for the school magazine about an unforgettable experience.
  • Write an article recommending a place to visit in Hong Kong.

文章结构（5段式）

1
Title（标题）
用疑问句、数字、惊叹句吸引眼球，如：Is Your Phone Ruining Your Sleep? 或 3 Reasons Why You Should Visit Sai Kung
2
Hook Introduction（吸引开头）
以问题、震撼数据、短故事或大胆陈述开篇。明确说明文章主题。
3
Body Paragraph 1（主体段1）
主题句 + 细节/例子/解释。每段只谈一个重点。
4
Body Paragraph 2（主体段2）
第二个重点，同样结构。可引用真实故事或数据增加说服力。
5
Conclusion（结论）
总结要点，呼吁行动或发人深省的结尾句（call to action / rhetorical question）。

Hook 开头4种方法

Rhetorical Question：Have you ever wondered why so many teenagers feel stressed all the time?
Shocking Statistic：Over 70% of Hong Kong students sleep fewer than eight hours a night.
Anecdote（小故事）：It was 2 a.m. when I finally put down my phone and realised I had wasted three hours scrolling.
Bold Statement：Social media is silently destroying our ability to focus.

文章写作注意事项

  • 写明刊物名称（如 the school magazine），增加真实感
  • 语气可以比 Essay 轻松，但不要太口语化
  • 每段要有主题句（topic sentence），不要离题
  • 结尾要有力，不要只写 "In conclusion, I hope you enjoyed reading this article."
  • 字数一般约 350–400 字

Essay 议论文写法

常见题型

  • Should students have smartphones at school? Write an essay expressing your views.
  • "Young people today are too dependent on technology." Do you agree? Write an essay.
  • Some people think exams should be replaced by other forms of assessment. Write an essay discussing this view.

论文结构（5段式 PEEL）

1
Introduction + Thesis（引言 + 立场）
引出背景 → 清晰表明你的立场（thesis statement）。如：While some argue that…, I believe that… because…
2
Body 1 — 论点一（最强论点）
Point → Evidence → Explain → Link back
3
Body 2 — 论点二
同样用 PEEL 结构，提出第二个理由
4
Counterargument + Rebuttal（反驳）
承认对立观点，再反驳。如：Admittedly, … However, …。这是 Level 5 的关键！
5
Conclusion（结论）
重申立场（用不同词汇），总结论点，可提出建议或展望。

PEEL 段落示范

Point To begin with, smartphones can be valuable educational tools.

Evidence For instance, students can use apps such as Quizlet to review vocabulary, or access reliable information online during research lessons.

Explain This means that, when used appropriately, technology can enhance learning rather than hinder it.

Link Therefore, a complete ban on smartphones may deprive students of a useful resource.

Counterargument 反驳模板

承认对方：Admittedly, / It is true that / Opponents may argue that / Some people believe that…

转折反驳：However, / Nevertheless, / Despite this, / Even so, / This does not mean that…

Thesis Statement 模板

Although there are [opposite view], I firmly believe that [your position] because [reason 1] and [reason 2].

In my opinion, [topic] should / should not [action] for the following reasons.

实用短语 Useful Phrases

开头句

In recent years, … has become a growing concern.
It is widely believed that…
Have you ever considered…?
We live in an age where…

递进 / 首先

First and foremost,
To begin with,
One significant reason is that
Above all,

递进 / 其次

Furthermore,
In addition to this,
What is more,
Another point worth noting is

举例

For instance,
To illustrate,
A case in point is…
Consider the example of…

反驳转折

Admittedly,
It is true that… However,
Nevertheless,
Despite this concern,

因果关系

As a result,
This leads to…
Consequently,
This means that…

结尾

In conclusion,
To sum up,
All things considered,
It is high time that…

❌ 避免这些常见错误

  • 不要写 "I think I think..." 重复
  • 避免 "very, very good / very very bad" 等重复副词
  • 不用 "In my humble opinion" — 太老套
  • 避免 "Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly, Lastly" 全部用 — 换点花样
  • 结尾不要只写 "That's all." 或 "Thank you for reading."（Article 结尾也不要）

练习题 Practice Questions

共6题，涵盖 Article 和 Essay 两种文体。点击"AI辅导"获取写作思路提示。

ARTICLE · Q1 · 难度：⭐⭐

Write an article for your school magazine about a habit that has improved your life. Explain what the habit is, how you developed it, and why you would recommend it to other students. (350 words)

提示 建议写：运动、阅读、早睡、冥想等。记得加 title 和 hook。

ARTICLE · Q2 · 难度：⭐⭐⭐

Write an article for a community newsletter about a local place in Hong Kong that deserves more attention. Describe the place and explain why residents should visit it. (350 words)

提示 可写：某条街、老市集、公园、博物馆等。要有具体细节，不要太笼统。

ARTICLE · Q3 · 难度：⭐⭐⭐

A local magazine is running a series called "Youth Voices". Write an article discussing the greatest challenge facing teenagers in Hong Kong today, and suggest ways to address it. (350–400 words)

提示 可选：学业压力、心理健康、就业前景、社交媒体影响。要提出具体建议。

ESSAY · Q4 · 难度：⭐⭐

"Students should not be given homework." Do you agree? Write an essay expressing your views. (350 words)

提示 记得写 thesis statement，加 counterargument 段落，不要只写单方面。

ESSAY · Q5 · 难度：⭐⭐⭐

"Young people spend too much time on social media, and this is harmful to their development." To what extent do you agree with this statement? Write an essay. (350–400 words)

提示 "To what extent" 表示你可以部分同意，要讨论两面，但要有清晰立场。

ESSAY · Q6 · 难度：⭐⭐⭐⭐

"Schools should focus more on students' mental health and wellbeing, even if it means spending less time on academic subjects." Write an essay discussing this view. (400 words)

提示 这道题是讨论型，需要平衡两面。可以先同意再反驳，结尾提出折中方案。

FORMAT 格式

Article：有标题，标题醒目有创意
注明文章刊登的媒体（如 school magazine）
Essay：首段有清晰 thesis statement
文章分段清晰，每段有缩进或空行

CONTENT 内容

每个主体段都有主题句 (topic sentence)
每个论点都有例子或解释支持
Essay：有 counterargument + rebuttal
结尾有力，不是草草收场
字数约达 350–400 字

LANGUAGE 语言

使用了不同的连接词（非只用 Firstly / Secondly）
词汇有变化，没有重复同一个词
检查主谓一致（he/she + verbs）
检查时态一致（全文统一用现在/过去）
没有不完整的句子（sentence fragment）
有至少一个复杂句式（relative clause / conditional）

💡 最后提醒

Level 5 的关键：词汇亮点 + 句式变化 + 有力结尾
如果时间不足，确保开头和结尾写好——考官最先和最后看的就是这两段
宁可字迹工整写350字，也不要潦草地写500字
